About me

Emory Andrew Tate III[8] was born on December 1, 1986,[9][10] in Walter Reed Army Medical Center in Washington, D.C.[11][12] He is mixed-race.[13] His African American father Emory Tate was a chess international master[14] and his mother worked as a catering assistant.[15] He has a brother, Tristan. He was raised in Chicago, Illinois and Goshen, Indiana. After his parents divorced, his mother brought both brothers to England.[16] Tate was raised in the Christian faith.[17] He learned to play chess at the age of five and competed in adult tournaments as a child.[
